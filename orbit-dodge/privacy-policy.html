Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Infinity Games ("we", "our", or "us") built Orbit Dodge as a free, ad-supported game. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Orbit Dodge does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game data (scores, settings) is stored locally on your device and is never sent to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display interstitial ads and rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage data

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data from apps.

Google Play Games Services

We use Google Play Games Services to provide leaderboard functionality. When you sign in, Google may collect:

  • Your Google Play Games profile information
  • Game scores submitted to leaderboards

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data through a third-party service, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Storage

All game-related data (high scores, settings) is stored locally on your device using local storage. This data is not transmitted to any external server. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

GDPR Consent (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you will be presented with a consent form before personalized ads are shown. You may choose to accept or decline personalized advertising. You can change your consent preferences at any time by clearing the app's data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
